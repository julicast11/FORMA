# FORMA

## Personal Performance OS

FORMA is a single-file, AI-powered health and performance dashboard built for athletes navigating high-demand lifestyles.

Designed around the reality of serious training combined with real life — early alarms, long commutes, injury management, and demanding work schedules — FORMA bridges the gap between **elite training discipline and adult life constraints.**

It is not a fitness tracker.  
It is the **decision layer between your body and your schedule.**

Garmin records the workout.  
FORMA plans the session.

---

# What It Does

FORMA acts as a **daily operating system for your body.**

Every morning it determines:

- when you should wake up
- what training session fits the day
- how to structure your time
- how recovery and injury status affect the plan

Wake-up time is calculated from:

- workout duration
- commute time (pool vs gym vs studio)
- morning routine
- work start time

Change any variable and the **entire week recalculates instantly.**

The goal is **clarity and consistency**, not more data.

# Key Features

## Intelligent Schedule Planning
Wake-up time is automatically calculated per day based on workout type, commute, routine length, and work start time.

## AI Dynamic Day Updates
When life changes, users simply explain the situation.

Examples:

> “Stuck at work until 8pm.”  
> “Shoulder is worse today.”  
> “I only have 30 minutes.”

The AI reads the user's profile, injuries, training plan, and available time, then restructures the day instantly.

## Dual Mode System
Beginner mode includes full exercise lists, sets, reps, and instructions.

Autonomous mode provides experienced athletes with a **clean framework card** — direction instead of step-by-step instructions.

After 28 consecutive active days, FORMA offers the transition automatically.

## Mandatory Warm-Up Protocol
Every session includes a warm-up timer with movements specific to the workout type.

Examples:

- lift days → dynamic activation
- swim days → shoulder mobility
- walk days → shin splint prevention

No session begins cold.

## Active Rehab Integration
Rehab exercises are integrated directly into warm-ups rather than treated as separate tasks.

Protocols include:

- shoulder rehabilitation
- shin splint prevention

Each includes tracking, streaks, and reasoning behind the movements.

## FORMA Assistant
A context-aware AI chat that already understands:

- HRV
- injury status
- weekly plan
- available time

Users receive relevant answers immediately without needing to explain context.

## Supplement Intelligence
Users can research supplements directly inside the system.

The AI returns:

- evidence-based explanation
- relevance to injuries and recovery
- interactions with current supplement stack
- personalized recommendations

# Goal Mix System

## Why It Exists

Most people are not training for a single goal.

They want to:

- perform well
- look good
- stay healthy
- remain consistent

The **Goal Mix system** allows users to select up to three priorities:

- Physique
- Performance
- Longevity

Users assign weights to each goal.

FORMA then adapts the **entire weekly plan** based on those priorities.

This transforms the system from a tracker into a **planning engine.**
